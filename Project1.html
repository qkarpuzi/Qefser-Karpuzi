Engineering behind Eli Proprete

Eli Proprete is a Kosovo‑based cleaning company that needed more than a brochure site — they needed a complete operational hub. I designed and built a system that handles online bookings, employee scheduling, customer management, and an admin dashboard with real‑time analytics.

The customer‑facing site is fully responsive and focuses on conversion: clear service presentation, easy booking flow, and automated email confirmations. The admin area (password protected) gives the owner full control over services, staff assignments, and financial overview.

Every part was built with security and maintainability in mind: prepared statements, input validation, and a clean separation of concerns.
