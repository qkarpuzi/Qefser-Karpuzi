Engineering V.Z-Entreprenor

V.Z-Entreprenor is a professional construction company based in Oslo, Norway. They needed a modern, responsive website to showcase their projects, services, and establish credibility in the competitive construction market.

I designed and built the site with a focus on clean aesthetics, professional presentation, and mobile responsiveness. The design emphasizes the company's portfolio while making it easy for potential clients to get in touch.

Every component was crafted with performance and maintainability in mind — from semantic HTML5 to optimized CSS and lightweight JavaScript interactions.
