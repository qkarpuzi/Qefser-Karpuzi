In the construction industry, credibility is built on precision. The objective for V.Z-Entreprenor was to build a digital storefront that reflects their physical craftsmanship.

I implemented a mobile-first responsive architecture using modern CSS3 Flexbox and Grid, ensuring that project managers on-site could access company details as easily as clients in the boardroom.
