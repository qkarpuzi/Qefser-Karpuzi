Engineering InnovaIT

InnovaIT is a modern, responsive website template designed for IT service companies and technology consultants. The template features a clean, professional design with a focus on showcasing services, expertise, and client success stories.

This template was designed with user experience in mind — intuitive navigation, clear calls‑to‑action, and a mobile‑first approach. The design emphasizes the company's expertise while maintaining a modern and professional aesthetic.

Built with semantic HTML5, CSS3 (Flexbox & Grid), and lightweight JavaScript for interactivity. Fully responsive and cross‑browser tested.
